Happy Independence Day

August 15th is celebrated as India's Independence Day, commemorating the nation's independence from the United Kingdom on this day in 1947. This day marks the end of British rule and the establishment of a free and sovereign Indian nation. It is a day of national pride, celebrated with flag-hoisting ceremonies, parades, and cultural events across the country.

Our Freedom Fighters

  • Mahatma Gandhi: The leader of India's non-violent independence movement against British rule, known for his philosophy of 'Satyagraha'.
  • Jawaharlal Nehru: The first Prime Minister of India, a key leader of the Indian National Congress and a central figure in the independence struggle.
  • Subhas Chandra Bose: A revolutionary nationalist who challenged Gandhi's non-violent methods and founded the Indian National Army (INA) to fight the British.
  • Bhagat Singh: A charismatic socialist revolutionary who played a significant role in the Indian independence movement and was executed by the British at a young age.
